Strategic EV Market Entry for 2025

The U.S. Electric Vehicle (EV) market is currently expanding but remains highly fragmented. While urban areas benefit from dense charging networks and high consumer readiness, rural markets struggle with infrastructure gaps, long travel distances, and specific performance needs related to climate and terrain. The goal of this project is to identify at least three high-potential U.S. regions for market entry by the end of 2025 and recommend strategies to increase adoption by 15%.
